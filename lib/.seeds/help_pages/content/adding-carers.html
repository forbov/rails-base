Adding Carers

A carer can perform the following:

  • To create shifts
  • To complete shift tasks
  • To view shift tasks

Carers can only be added by a Client. To add a carer click the 'Add Carer' button from the Carers tab on the Dashboard screen.

The following fields are mandatory:

  • Email: Must be a valid email address and acts as a login so must be unique within the system
  • First and Last Name
  • Mobile Phone
  • Gender

Once the carer is defined an invitation will be sent to them for them to join and they will be associated with the client who invited them.
