Completing Tasks

Tasks can only be completed by Carers. To complete a task do the following:

  • Navigate to the Clients tab on the Dashboard screen.
  • Click on the 'Shift Tasks' button next to the client you wish to start the shift for
  • Open one of the listed tasks yet to be completed
  • Click the checkbox once you have completed the task
  • Log any notes to communicate back to your client. eg: of supplies are running short etc.

If a shift has been started already for a client clicking the 'Shift Tasks' button will open tasks for that shift.
